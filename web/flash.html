WEB FLASHER — HELTEC WIFI LORA 32 V3 (ESP32-S3)

Flash your device

  1. Use desktop Chrome or Edge (Web Serial isn't supported on Firefox, Safari, or mobile).
  2. Connect the Heltec V3 over USB-C.
  3. Click Install, pick the serial port, and confirm.

Your browser can't flash — use desktop Chrome or Edge. Open this page over HTTPS to flash.

On a fresh device, accept the erase prompt for a clean install — this wipes stored config and BLE pairings, and the device boots into the "Argus Setup" onboarding AP. Re-flashing an already-configured unit without erasing keeps its config.

After flashing

  1. Join the Argus Setup WiFi, open http://192.168.4.1, choose Mobile or Base.
  2. Mobile units stream to a phone over Bluetooth — open the BLE client.
